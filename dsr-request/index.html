Monthly Services — How This Works

Thanks for choosing The Dev Side for your ongoing website maintenance, Facebook marketing, and graphic design needs. Below is a walkthrough of what's included and how we'll work together each month.

1

Website Maintenance

Up to 2 hours per month of content updates on your existing Wix site — text changes, image swaps, and minor layout adjustments. Just send requests via email or text and we'll handle it.

2

Facebook Marketing

We'll create and schedule 1 post per week (4–5 per month) on your Facebook page. You provide the photos and videos — we write the post copy, create graphics, and handle scheduling, comments, and messages.

3

Quarterly Flyer Design

One professionally designed flyer per quarter (4 per year). Each flyer includes an initial concept plus 2 rounds of revisions. Perfect for seasonal promotions, events, or sales.

4

Submit Requests

All requests should be submitted in writing via email (info@thedevside.com) or text message. We respond within 2 business days, Monday–Friday, 8 AM–5 PM CST.

5

Monthly Billing

$250/month billed on the 1st, due within 15 days. Any work beyond the included hours is billed at $75/hr with your written approval before we begin overage work.

What We'll Need From You

📷
Photos & Videos: For Facebook posts, provide photos and/or videos at least 5 business days before the scheduled post date. For flyers, provide details at least 14 business days before you need them.
📝
Written Requests: All requests must be submitted in writing via email or text. Verbal requests (phone, in-person) need to be followed up in writing to be actioned.
💬
Timely Approvals: When we send you something to review, please respond within 5 business days. If we don't hear back, the work is considered approved.
🔑
Wix Account: You're responsible for maintaining your Wix account and hosting. We handle content updates, but Wix billing and platform uptime are on your end.

Questions? Reach out anytime — no pressure.

1. Scope of Services

The Designer agrees to provide the following ongoing monthly services to the Client:

A. Website Maintenance — $75.00/month

Up to two (2) hours per month of content updates on the Client's existing Wix website (dakotasalesandrental.com), including:

  • Updating text on existing pages
  • Swapping or adding images on existing pages
  • Minor formatting and layout adjustments within existing page structures
Website Maintenance Does NOT Include: Creating new pages or sections; redesigning existing pages or layouts; adding new functionality, features, or plugins; platform migrations or changes; troubleshooting issues caused by the Client, third parties, or Wix platform changes. These items are treated as Change Orders (see Section 3).

B. Facebook Marketing — $100.00/month

The Designer will create and publish one (1) post per week (approximately 4–5 posts per month) on the Client's Facebook business page, subject to the following:

  • Designer writes post copy (captions, descriptions, text)
  • Designer creates graphics for posts as needed
  • Designer schedules and publishes posts
  • Designer manages comments, messages, and community engagement on the Client's Facebook page
  • Client provides photos and/or videos at least 5 business days before the scheduled post date
  • If Client fails to provide photos/videos by the deadline, the post for that week may be skipped at the Designer's discretion without reduction in the monthly fee
Facebook Marketing Does NOT Include: Creating photos or videos (Client must provide these); managing paid advertising or boosted posts; running ad campaigns; analytics reporting; managing any social media platform other than Facebook.

C. Graphic Design (Flyer) — $75.00/month

The Designer will create one (1) flyer design per quarter (4 flyers per year), billed monthly as part of the total monthly fee. Each flyer includes:

  • Initial design concept based on Client-provided content and direction
  • Two (2) rounds of revisions per flyer
  • Final deliverable in PDF and/or print-ready format
  • Client must provide all flyer details, content, and direction at least 14 business days before the flyer is needed
Graphic Design Does NOT Include: Additional flyers beyond 1 per quarter; revision rounds beyond the 2 included; design work other than flyers (brochures, business cards, logos, etc.); photography or image creation. Additional flyers or extra revision rounds are billed as Change Orders at $75.00/hour.

Included Changes (Within Scope — No Additional Charge)

  • Swapping images on existing website pages
  • Minor formatting adjustments on existing pages

  • Managing comments, messages, and engagement on the Client's Facebook page
  • Flyer revisions within the 2 included rounds

Change Orders (Billed Separately at $75.00/hour)

  • Creating new website pages or sections
  • Adding new functionality, features, or plugins to the website
  • Redesigning existing website pages or layouts
  • Any website work exceeding the 2 included hours per month
  • Additional flyers beyond 1 per quarter
  • Flyer revisions beyond the 2 included rounds per flyer
  • Creating photos or videos (Client must provide these)
  • Troubleshooting website issues not caused by Designer
  • Emergency or rush requests with less than 48 hours notice

The Client must approve the additional work and estimated cost in writing (via email or text message) before the Designer proceeds. Unapproved work will not be performed.
